fallen

Print

Model: fallen

Auxiliary: sein

Other forms: nicht fallen

Irregular conjugation for ther verb "fallen" and its compounds: stem vowel mutation (umlaut) in singular form of the second and third person in Present tense ("du fällst").There is a vowel change in the stem of Present tense ("ich falle") and the stem of Preterite ("ich fiel") and the stem of Past Participle ("gefallen").

The verb has several variants of conjugation, which may correspond to different meanings. Please use the menu to select one or all variants.
